# Idea - Grab Discussion Wrap-Up Topics

## The Idea
Prepare a structured set of talking points for any Grab conversation — covering collaboration angles, integration possibilities, and how to close the discussion with clear next steps.

## Talking Points

### Context Setting
- What ALVA does and where Grab fits in (mobility, logistics, fintech — overlap areas)
- Current pain points we're solving internally that Grab might relate to
- What prompted this conversation now

### Collaboration Angles
- **GrabCar / GrabBike corporate** — fleet or ride-hailing for ALVA staff / operational needs
- **OVO / GrabPay** — exploring as a payment rail for ALVA customers or internal disbursements
- **GrabExpress / GrabKilat** — on-demand delivery for ALVA's logistics use cases
- **GrabAds / data** — any audience or mobility data partnership potential
- **B2B / GrabForBusiness** — corporate accounts, billing consolidation, expense management
- **Automation touchpoints** — Grab API / webhook integrations into ALVA's Lark-based workflows

### Things to Clarify from Their Side
- Who is the right point of contact post-meeting (BD, tech, product)?
- What's their current B2B partnership process / onboarding timeline?
- Any existing integrations with companies similar to ALVA?
- NDAs or partnership agreements needed before going deeper?
- How does GrabForBusiness pricing work at ALVA's scale?

### Wrap-Up Checklist
- [ ] Summarize what was agreed or explored
- [ ] Confirm next step owner on both sides
- [ ] Set a follow-up date / channel (email, Lark, WhatsApp?)
- [ ] Identify any materials to share (deck, API docs, use case brief)
- [ ] Clarify if any internal approval is needed before moving forward

## Why It Matters
Grab has strong regional infrastructure across payments (OVO), logistics (GrabExpress), and corporate mobility that could reduce operational overhead for ALVA. A well-structured wrap-up keeps the conversation moving rather than stalling after the first meeting.
