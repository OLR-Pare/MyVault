# Idea - Gojek Discussion Wrap-Up Topics

## The Idea
Prepare a structured set of talking points to make the most of any Gojek conversation — covering collaboration angles, integration possibilities, and how to close the discussion with clear next steps.

## Talking Points

### Context Setting
- What ALVA does and where Gojek fits in (fleet ops, mobility, delivery — overlap areas)
- Current pain points we're solving internally that Gojek might relate to
- What prompted this conversation now

### Collaboration Angles
- **GoFleet / logistics API** — can we plug into Gojek's fleet or driver network for ALVA use cases?
- **GoPay / payment rails** — exploring GoPay as a payment method for ALVA customers or internal reimbursements
- **Data / analytics partnership** — shared mobility data, urban movement patterns, anything mutually useful
- **B2B / corporate accounts** — does Gojek have a corporate product (GoKilat, GoFood B2B) that ALVA could use at scale?
- **Automation touchpoints** — Gojek webhooks, API integrations into ALVA's Lark-based workflows

### Things to Clarify from Their Side
- Who is the right point of contact post-meeting (BD, tech, product)?
- What's their current B2B partnership process / onboarding timeline?
- Any existing integrations they've done with companies similar to ALVA?
- NDAs or partnership agreements needed before going deeper?

### Wrap-Up Checklist
- [ ] Summarize what was agreed or explored
- [ ] Confirm next step owner on both sides
- [ ] Set a follow-up date / channel (email, Lark, WhatsApp?)
- [ ] Identify any materials to share (deck, API docs, use case brief)
- [ ] Clarify if any internal approval is needed before moving forward

## Why It Matters
Gojek has infrastructure (payments, logistics, driver network, data) that could accelerate ALVA's operations without building from scratch. A well-structured wrap-up ensures the conversation converts to action rather than going cold.
