# AgencyOS

## Goal

Build a front-facing Lark Base app suite that gives agency teams complete visibility into their business: pipeline, sales, payments, invoicing, client satisfaction, and team happiness.

## What It Includes

- **Pipeline Tracker** — visibility into deal flow and sales pipeline
- **Sales Tracker** — track sales activities and outcomes
- **Payment Tracker** — monitor incoming payments from clients
- **Invoice Tracker** — unified invoice management
- **Client NPS** — measure and track client satisfaction
- **Employee Happiness** — track team morale and engagement
