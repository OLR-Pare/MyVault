# FinanceOS

## Goal

Build a front-facing Lark Base app suite that automates and streamlines core finance processes: accounts receivable, accounts payable, cashflow visibility, and approval workflows.

## What It Includes

- **AR (Accounts Receivable)** — track and manage customer invoices and payments
- **AP (Accounts Payable)** — manage vendor bills and payment schedules
- **Cashflow** — real-time cashflow forecasting and visibility
- **Approval Process** — automated approval workflows for financial transactions
